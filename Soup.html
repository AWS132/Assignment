Tomato Soup

Description

A simple 5 ingredient tomato soup.

Original recipe here by Lindsay on pinchofyum.com

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes.
  • Butter.
  • A little onion.
  • Garlic.
  • Salt.

Steps

  1. Put the tomatoes, butter, and onion in a large saucepan or Dutch oven. Bring it up to a low bubble, then turn the heat to medium low. Cover partially with a lid; let it simmer for 45 minutes. Stir every 15 minutes or so to prevent scorching on the bottom.

  2. Remove the onion pieces using tongs.

  3. Blend the Tomato Soup

  4. Serve tomato soup with garlic bread, grilled cheese, croutons, crackers, pesto, whatever you like!
