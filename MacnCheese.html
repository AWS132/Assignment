Mac and Cheese

Description

Instant Pot Mac and Cheese.

Original recipe here by Lindsay on pinchofyum.com

Ingredients

  • 1 pound uncooked elbow pasta
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons butter, cut into small pieces
  • 2 1/2 cups shredded cheese
  • up to 1/2 cup whole milk

Steps

  1. Put noodles, water, and salt into the Instant Pot, plop the lid on to seal it, and push a few buttons. Let the whole thing pressure cook in the Instant Pot under high pressure.

  2. Stir in butter, cheese, and milk until it all melts to make your cheese sauce.
